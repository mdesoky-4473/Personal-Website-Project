Momentum-based ETF

The BITA Desoky World Dynamic Momentum Leaders Index aims to capture the performance of a price momentum equity strategy by systematically identifying and investing in high-performing stocks from 23 developed markets. The index applies quality screens to the investment universe and subsequently selects the top 100 components with the highest price momentum. This approach provides a disciplined way to capitalize on growth opportunities and retain high responsiveness to market trends. Index constituents are weighted by free-float market capitalization, and the index is rebalanced on a quarterly basis.
